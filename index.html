Local Activities

Available Tours

Whale Watching Tour - Duration 60min

Embark on an exciting whale watching tour off the coast of Galle, where you can witness these majestic creatures in their natural habitat.

Enjoy the breathtaking views of the ocean and the opportunity to see a variety of marine life, including dolphins and sea turtles.

Galle Fort Walking Tour - Duration 90min

Take a guided walking tour through the historic Galle Fort, learning about its rich history, cultural significance, and colonial architecture.

Explore the narrow streets, visit museums, and enjoy the vibrant atmosphere of this unique UNESCO World Heritage site.

Boat Safari on Madu River - Duration 60min

Enjoy a serene boat safari on the Madu River, a biodiverse estuary known for its mangrove forests, birdlife, and small islands.

This tour offers a tranquil escape with opportunities to see wildlife up close and experience the natural beauty of the area.

Unawatuna Beach Relaxation - Duration 60min

Unwind at the picturesque Unawatuna Beach, known for its golden sands, clear waters, and vibrant coral reefs.

This tour provides a perfect getaway for sunbathing, swimming, and enjoying the scenic coastal beauty of Galle.
